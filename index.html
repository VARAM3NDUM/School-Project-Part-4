The requisite coursework should fortify the intrinsic passion a teacher has for that subject specialty. For example, if a teacher is teaching physics or calculus, he should have a respectable first degree in those subjects, preferably with honors. Adding a masters degree in those disciplines completes the package. A terminal degree is nice but not required.
